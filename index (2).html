Our Story

A small kitchen with a big following.

A Taste of India is a popular neighborhood restaurant offering a variety of Indian dishes at genuinely affordable prices — and a loyal customer base that keeps praising the consistent quality.

It's a cozy spot with a limited but well-curated menu — perfect for a simple, tasty meal without spending too much. With options for non-veg, veg, and vegan diners, the combos are a great value with generous portion sizes.

Fresh, flavorful food at reasonable prices: that's the whole idea.
